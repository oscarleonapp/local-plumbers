What is the Moen Flo Device?

The Moen Flo Device is more than just a smart water shutoff valve; it's a comprehensive water monitoring and control system designed to protect your home from water damage. Equipped with advanced sensors and intelligent algorithms, the Moen Flo constantly monitors your home's water usage, detecting leaks, and abnormalities in real-time.

Key Features:

1. 24/7 Monitoring:

The Moen Flo Device keeps a watchful eye on your home's water usage round the clock, alerting you to any irregularities via the accompanying smartphone app.

2. Automatic Shutoff:

In the event of a detected leak or burst pipe, the Moen Flo can automatically shut off your home's water supply, minimizing potential damage.

3. Customizable Alerts:

Tailor alerts to your preferences, ensuring you're always informed about what's happening with your home's water system.

3. Remote Access:

Access your Moen Flo system from anywhere using the intuitive smartphone app, giving you peace of mind even when you're away from home.
